Privacy Policy

JaipurRide - Jaipur Metro Guide

Effective Date: 13 March 2026

Introduction

JaipurRide (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the JaipurRide mobile application and related services.

JaipurRide is a travel companion designed to help users navigate the Jaipur Metro system and discover nearby tourist attractions across Jaipur.

Information We Collect

JaipurRide is designed to work primarily offline and does not require user accounts.

  • We do not collect personal information such as name, email address or phone number.
  • The app may access your device location to show nearby metro stations.
  • Location data is used only within the app and is not stored on any server.

Location Usage

Location access is used only for features such as:

  • Finding the nearest metro station
  • Showing directions to metro stations
  • Discovering nearby tourist attractions

Your location is processed locally on your device and is never stored or transmitted by JaipurRide.

Local Data Storage

Some data may be stored locally on your device for better user experience:

  • Selected metro stations
  • Route history
  • Application preferences
  • Offline metro station data

This information remains on your device and is not shared with external servers.

Third Party Services

JaipurRide may provide links that open external services such as Google Maps for navigation.

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

JaipurRide does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us.

Security

Since JaipurRide does not store personal data on servers, risks related to data breaches are minimized.

All processing happens locally on the device wherever possible.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
